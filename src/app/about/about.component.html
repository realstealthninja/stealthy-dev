Who am I?

I am a 17 Year old from Kerala, Passionate about literature and poetry. I play videogames sometimes, and I code too! I've been coding since 2019 and got my start in Roblox and Lua.
I love mathematics, It's one of if not my favorite subjects. My favorite mathematican has to be Leonhard Euler. His life is of great inspiration for me. I consider mathematics and programming to be an art just as poetry and literature. I think math is really beautiful.
